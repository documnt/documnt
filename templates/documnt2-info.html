DOCUMNT2

Second print issue of DOCUMNT artists magazine / book dedicated to conceptual writing and "concrete poetry".

With an unstable focus on the changing materialities and temporalities of “archives”, DOCUMNT2 contains essays, interviews and artworks interspersed with the conditions of its production (chats, notes, scans, screenshots).

With contributions by:
Irena Hollowell, Johannes Wilke, Hanne Lippard, Ella Coon, aaaaarg.fail, Hito Steyerl, Renata Adler, Grischa Lichtenberger, Nico Jungel & burgund t brandt, Daniel Temkin, Anna Erdmann, Anja Kaiser, Experimental Jetset, Jake Reber, Ewa Wójtowicz, Inside Job, Sarah Newman, Jessica Yurkofsky & Rachel Kalmar, Malin Gewinner, Lynne Huffer and Mark Leckey

DOCUMNT2 was designed by Johannes Wilke in collaboration with co-founding editors Matt Arnold and Anne Lippert.

Released 2018-07-06
Dimensions 23 x 16 cm
208 pages
