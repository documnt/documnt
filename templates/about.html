Documnt is a
hybrid archive-
publishing
project:
/ magazine
/ theory journal
/ artists book
/ mixtape series
started by Matt
Arnold and Anne
Lippert in 2015.
It’s grown into
an international
community of
artists, writers,
musicians, & djs
between Berlin,
New York City,
& Vancouver

As experiments
in post-internet
documentation, or
the possibilities
of handling info
analog/digital
we are motivated
against individualist
or quantitative-
obsessed logic,
toward the
egalitarian and
emancipatory
These are vibrant
times

We are always
accepting
submissions of
artworks, texts
and mixtapes
